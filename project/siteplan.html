Danny Online Stores

This name represents an online shopping site where users can order food items and make payments

Site Purpose

It's purpose is to offer an online food order platform where users can seamlessly order available food items and make payments for their order which will be delivered in their destinations

Scenario

  • How many items can I buy to get a discount
  • Do I pay for the delivery too?

Color Schema/Typography

  • Dusted-Olive - Header and Footer
  • Muted-Olive-2 - nav a:hover
  • Amaranth - body
