Nigeria - The Giant of Africa

Nigeria is a country located in the West of Africa. It is called the Giant of Africa because of its large population which is over 200 million people, making it the most populous country in Africa and the seventh most populous country in the world. Nigeria is known for its diverse culture, rich history, and vibrant economy. The country is a major producer of oil and natural gas, which are significant contributors to its economy. Nigeria is also known for its music, film industry (Nollywood), and literature, producing internationally acclaimed artists and authors.
